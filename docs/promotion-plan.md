# TradingVerse — Promotions Plan

## Short-Form Video (YouTube Shorts + Instagram Reels)

### Why it works
- Trading content performs extremely well on both platforms — high-intent, high-engagement audience
- Screen recordings of a real, data-dense product are more convincing than any written copy
- "Pre-market routine" and "before I trade" hooks go viral in trading communities
- 30–60 second format is ideal for showing one feature end-to-end

---

### Video Ideas

#### Video 1 — "What I check before 9:15 AM" (30s)
**Hook:** *"Most traders open the market blind. Here's what I check first."*

**Script/flow:**
1. Open shot: phone/screen showing 8:50 AM timestamp
2. Screen record pre-market page loading
3. Text overlay as each section appears:
   - Global cues → *"What happened overnight"*
   - Key levels → *"Where the action will be"*
   - AI Week Plan → *"What smart money expects"*
4. End card: *"TradingVerse — free at tradingverse.in"*

**No voiceover needed** — punchy text overlays + lofi background music

---

#### Video 2 — "Before I place any trade, 4 agents review it" (45s)
**Hook:** *"I stopped trading on instinct. This is what replaced it."*

**Script/flow:**
1. Open: show a trade setup on screen (NIFTY option)
2. Open Order Intelligence panel
3. Watch each agent fire — Trend ✓, Volatility ✓, Behavioural ✓, Order Flow ✓
4. Scenario card appears — Bull 52%, Base 30%, Bear 18%
5. Text overlay: *"Now I know exactly what I'm walking into"*
6. End card: *"Order Intelligence — free tier: 3/day"*

---

#### Video 3 — "Can you read this chart?" (30s)
**Hook:** *"Test your trading instincts."*

**Script/flow:**
- Show scenario game: market context cues appear → question pops up → 3 options
- User picks wrong → explanation appears → correct answer revealed with reasoning
- Repeat with a second scenario
- End: *"5 games. Free. No signup needed."*

**Why this works:** Interactive challenge format does huge numbers. Viewers pause to think before the reveal.

---

#### Video 4 — "My pre-market to live trade workflow" (60s)
**Hook:** *"Entire trading workflow in 60 seconds."*

**Script/flow:**
1. 8:50 AM — pre-market: global cues + key levels (10s)
2. 9:15 AM — terminal opens, chart + option chain side by side (10s)
3. Chartink alert fires → stock auto-appears in watchlist (10s)
4. Order Intelligence runs on the setup (10s)
5. Order placed via Kite, position visible (10s)
6. Market commentary update: regime badge + breadth (10s)
7. End: *"TradingVerse — from context to execution"*

---

#### Video 5 — "The stat that changed how I trade" (20s)
**Hook:** *"83% of retail traders lose money."*

**Script/flow:**
- Big "83%" text on screen
- *"The 17% who win? They understand context before placing a trade."*
- Quick cuts: pre-market → terminal → commentary → Order Intelligence
- *"TradingVerse. Free to start."*

### Production Notes

**Tools:**
- Screen record with QuickTime (macOS) or OBS — 1440×900, then crop/resize to 9:16 for Shorts/Reels
- Add text overlays in CapCut or DaVinci Resolve (free)
- Background music: lofi or minimal — no voiceover needed for most videos
- Add captions — both platforms auto-boost captioned videos

**When to record:**
- Videos 1, 2, 4 — best recorded during market hours with live data visible
- Videos 3, 5 — can be recorded any time (no live data needed)

**Posting cadence:**
- Start with 1–2/week
- Best times for Indian trading audience: 8–9 AM (pre-market) or 7–9 PM (evening review time)
- Use hashtags: #NiftyTrading #OptionsTrading #IndianStockMarket #TradingTips #Zerodha
